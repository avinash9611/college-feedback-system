About

• Student Feedback System for college students have been developed which aims to rate and analyze the college’s faculty performance.
• This system is designed to reduce the workload of physically examining the feedback pages of every student.
• The system also reduces the burden of efforts being put in keeping and maintaining the records on a manual base which also requires a lot of space and safety measures in place.
• Also further enhancement can be done and more features can be added for better retrieval of the feedback details.
